Basic Information

Project Name: Pantry and Kitchen Interior Design for Mr. Naresh Reddy

Client: Mr. Naresh Reddy

Location: Shollinganallur

Scope: Interior Design

Project Type: Residential Kitchen and Pantry Design

Scale: Custom-designed based on client requirements

Project Overview

This project involves a complete interior design solution for a modern pantry and kitchen space. The design focuses on functionality, aesthetics, and efficient space utilization while incorporating high-quality materials and ergonomic layouts.

Key Features

  • Modular Kitchen Setup with smart storage solutions
  • Sink Unit & Refrigerator Area for optimized workflow
  • Tall Units with Microwave Oven for space efficiency
  • Pull-Down Shelves for better accessibility
  • Oil Pull-Out and Tandem Box Drawer Units for organized storage
  • Custom Cabinetry & Chimney Setup for a seamless look
