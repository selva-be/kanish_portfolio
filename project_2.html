Project Details

Basic Information

Project: Bespoke Modular Wardrobes

Client: M/S_Designran

Site Location: Olympia Penache, Navalur

Project Type: Residential

Scale: 1mm : 50mm

Project Overview

This project involves the design and execution of bespoke modular wardrobes for different bedrooms in a residential space. Each wardrobe is custom-built to maximize functionality while maintaining aesthetic appeal.

Wardrobe Designs

1. Guest Bedroom Wardrobe

Design Features:

  • Combination of fabric laminate and PU finish.
  • Integrated loft storage.
  • Chest of drawers with internal organization.
  • Architray detailing for an enhanced aesthetic appeal.
  • Strip light provision for better visibility.

Sections Included: Plan & Section Views, External Elevation A & B, Internal Elevation A & B

2. Senior Bedroom Wardrobe

Design Features:

  • Matte/Suede laminate finish with veneer detailing.
  • Tip-on loft handles for a sleek design.
  • Finger groove shutters for handleless aesthetics.
  • Side dummy panels to ensure a flush look.
  • Integrated shelving and drawers for optimized storage.

Sections Included: Plan @ 600mm & 3000mm, External Elevation, Internal Elevation, Section A

3. Master Bedroom Wardrobe

Design Features:

  • Open shelving with PU finish for display units.
  • Rounded-edge wooden shelves for a soft, modern touch.
  • Dedicated space for printer use.
  • Hidden storage compartments for better organization.
  • Hanging rods at different levels for optimized clothing storage.

Sections Included: Plan Layout, External Elevation A, Internal Elevation A

Material Selection

External Finishes: PU Finish, Veneer, Fabric Laminate

Internal Finishes: Matte Laminate, Wooden Shelves

Additional Features: Dummy Panels, Loft Storage, LED Strip Lights
